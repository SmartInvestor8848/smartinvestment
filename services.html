Established in 2020 for sharing financial Advices. Stocks can be a valuable part ofyour investment portfolio. Owning stocks in different companies can help you build your savings, protect your money from inflation and maximize income from your investments. It's important to know that there are risks when investing in thestock market. Like any investment, it helps to understand the risk/return relationship and your own tolerance for risk.

Historically, long-term equity returns have been better than returns from cash or fixed-income investments such as bonds. However, stock prices tend to rise and fall over time. Investors may want to consider a long-term perspective for their equity portfolio because these stock-market fluctuations do tend to smooth outoverlonger periods of time.
